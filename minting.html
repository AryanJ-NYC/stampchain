Minting

  1. Login to Counterwallet. This is important as Freewallet imposes an artificial limit on the length of the description string.
  2. Create a numeric asset (not named). The reason for this is largely philosophical as we are trying to position our project as "Bitcoin-only" and numeric assets do not require xcp to create.
  3. Convert your art into base64. Here is a tool: https://codebeautify.org/gif-to-base64-converter
  4. Prepend STAMP: before the base64 string. Eg: STAMP:ZyXqwer....
  5. Put the long string into the Description field.
  6. Check the mempool. Probably need to do 15-20 sats/byte for a 24 hour confirmation. Higher if you want it confirmed faster.
  7. Your STAMP will appear in the directory chronologically.
